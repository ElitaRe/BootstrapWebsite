Menu

We serve different kinds of drinks, just depends on your taste!

GIN HENDRICKS

The "unexpected" GIN HENDRICK’S & TONIC mixing of cucumber and rose petals , result in a most iconoglastic gin and more refreshnes for all days.

TERESITA

A Teresita Summer Cocktail comes with a blend of vodka and orange juice blend with orange marmalade & fresh mint.

PINA COLADA

Pina Colada is a Cocktail from Puerto Rico, a sweet cocktail made with rum, cream of coconut or coconut milk and pineapple juice, served with fruit.

MARGARITA

According to cocktail historian David Wondrich, Margarita is related to a popular Mexican and American drink!
